Sweet-potato-soup
It is very easy and always turns out well.
Ingredience
  • 1(200~250g) sweet potato
  • 1/4 onion
  • 10g butter
  • 1 bouillon cube
  • 400cc milk
  • 200cc water
  • salt (to taste)
  • black peppar (to taste)
  • heavy cream for decoration
Prework
  • Slice the onions.
  • Peel the sweet potato and slice them into 3 cm slices.
Steps
  1. Add butter in the deep fry pan.Let it melt and add some cliced onions.
  2. Stir until the onions get transparent.
  3. Add sweet potatos and stir them well.
  4. Add water and bouillon. Put on the lid and let it cook for about 20 minutes on low heat.
  5. Check if the sweet potatos are soft. Use a hand mixer to mix them until it is smooth. Add 200cc of milk if it is difficult to mix.
  6. Pour the mixture back to the deepfry pan. Turn on the heat let it warm up again and add the remaining milk.

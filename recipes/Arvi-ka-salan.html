Arvi-ka-salan

Arvi-ka-salan~Taro curry
It is avegetarian dish with lots of spices.Enjoy.
Ingredience
  • taro 250g
  • onion 2
  • tomato 1
  • oil 2 table spoons
  • turmeric powder 1 small table spoon
  • chilli powder 1 small table spoon
  • chinamon stick 1
  • water 1 cup
  • cuminpowder 1 tea spoon
  • dry red chilli 1
  • garam masala 1 small table spoon
  • garlic 2 cloves
  • ginger 1 table spoon
  • lemon juice 1 tea spoon
  • coriander 1 bunch

Welsh-onion-grill-with-spicy-sauce
You want a tasty,easy and quick-to-make side dish? Here it is!
Ingredience
  • 2 Welsh onion
  • 1 tablespoon sesami oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon soysauce
  • 1 teaspoon ra-yu(chilli sauce)
  • 2 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon white sesami seeds
Prework
  • Cut the Welsh leek into 5 cm slices.
  • Mix all the rest of the ingredience except for the sesami oil.
Steps
  1. Pour sesami oil into a fry pan. Put in the welsh onions and cook them until both sides get little bit brown.
  2. Take the welsh onions out. Pour all the rest of the ingredience int to the same pan. Let it boil.
  3. Pour it on top of the welsh onions.
